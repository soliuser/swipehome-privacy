Privacy Policy

SwipeHome · Last updated: March 26, 2026

SwipeHome ("the app", "we", "us") is a mobile application for browsing real estate listings. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

  • Account data: your name, email address, and profile picture retrieved from your Google account upon sign-in.
  • Property data (sellers only): title, description, price, location, and images you upload.
  • Messages: text messages exchanged between buyers and sellers within the app.
  • Device location (optional): GPS coordinates used solely to show distance to nearby listings in the current session. Location is never stored on our servers.
  • Technical data: app version and device type (anonymized, for debugging purposes).
  • Payment data: transaction records processed via Stripe. We do not store card numbers or sensitive payment details — these are handled entirely by Stripe.

2. Sign-in via Google

We use Google Sign-In for authentication. Upon sign-in, Google shares your public profile information (name, email, profile picture) with us. Your Google password is never accessible to us. Authentication and session management are handled by Supabase Auth.

3. Data Storage — Supabase

Your data is stored in a secure database provided by Supabase (PostgreSQL). Each user can access only their own data, enforced by Row Level Security (RLS) policies. Property images are stored in Supabase Storage.

4. Local Data (on your device)

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Your list of saved (favorite) properties (AsyncStorage)
  • Active search filters (AsyncStorage)
  • Session token for automatic sign-in

This data remains only on your device and is cleared when you sign out.

5. Payments — Stripe

Payments are processed securely by Stripe. When you make a payment, your card details are entered directly into Stripe's secure interface and never transmitted to our servers. We store only transaction status and Stripe's payment intent ID for record-keeping. For more information, see Stripe's Privacy Policy.

6. How We Use Your Data

  • To display your profile and identify you in conversations
  • To allow you to publish and manage property listings (sellers)
  • To enable communication between buyers and sellers
  • To show distance to properties (processed locally, not sent to our servers)
  • To process payments for listing fees and premium subscriptions

7. Sharing Data with Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. We use the following trusted services to operate the app:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (privacy policy)
  • Google Sign-In — authentication (privacy policy)
  • Stripe — payment processing (privacy policy)
  • Firebase — analytics and push notifications (privacy policy)

8. Data Security

All data in transit is protected by TLS encryption (HTTPS). Database access is regulated by RLS policies — each user can only read and write their own data. Property images are publicly readable (intended for display), but writing or deleting requires authentication.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we have stored about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Revoke Google account access via Google Account Settings

To delete your account, use the option in the app (Profile → Delete Account) or contact us at the email below.

10. Children

This app is not intended for persons under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes within the app. The date of the last update is shown at the top of this page.
